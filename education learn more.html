Education & Empowerment Project

Digital Skills for Tomorrow

Providing basic computer access and digital literacy training to prepare older children for modern jobs and opportunities.

The Need for Digital Literacy

In the Mtwara region, access to technology and quality digital education is extremely limited. Without these foundational skills, vulnerable children face enormous barriers to higher education and meaningful employment in an increasingly digitized world. Our program aims to close this gap, transforming potential into opportunity.

What We Do

  • **Basic Computer Access:** Setting up dedicated computer stations for hands-on learning.
  • **Core Digital Literacy:** Teaching essential skills like word processing, internet navigation, and online safety.
  • **Future-Ready Training:** Introducing concepts like graphic design basics and digital communication platforms crucial for the job market.

Our Expected Impact

120+ Children

Receiving certified training annually.

40% Higher

Projected employment rate after graduation.
