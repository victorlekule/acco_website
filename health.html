YOUTH, CHILDREN HEALTH & NUTRITION INITIATIVES

At AFRICAN CHILD COMFORTERS ORGANIZATION, we believe that a healthy child is a child who can learn, lead, and dream. This initiative focuses on the physical and emotional well-being of children and youth across Tanzania, with a special focus on supporting the unique needs of girls and vulnerable students. By partnering with different centers we bridge the gap between community needs and professional healthcare.

What We Do

  • Menstrual Hygiene & Health We empower adolescent girls by providing menstrual hygiene education and sanitary supplies to ensure that no girl misses school because of her period.
  • Inclusive Education & Special needs support We are dedicated to supporting children with diverse needs, ensuring they have a comfortable environment to thrive.
  • Life Saving Health Drives At ACCO, we don't just stand by; we take action. Our blood donation projects bring the community to the frontlines of healthcare, supporting hospitals and saving the lives of mothers and kids in their most critical moments.
  • Mental Health & Peer Support For us, health is more than just medicine, it’s about the mind too. We create supportive environments where youth can discuss the challenges of growing up today, helping them stay away from drugs and stay focused on their education and dreams.
  • HIV & Substance Abuse Awareness We are committed to protecting our youth from the risks of drug abuse and HIV. Through school outreach and "Safe Space" dialogues, we provide the right information to help young people make healthy choices, resist peer pressure, and understand the real impact of substance use on their lives and futures.
